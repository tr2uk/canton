STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES

Current Challenges

  • Manual Forms: Paper entries for tournaments (e.g., Kent Open) are inefficient and prone to data loss.
  • Uniform Issues: Standard Gi sizes often don't fit, leading students to buy non-branded gear externally. Lost revenue.
  • Hidden Churn: Students facing financial hardship leave silently because asking for help is difficult.

Proposed Solutions

  • The "Patch Model": Allow students to buy high-quality WKF gear anywhere, but mandate a "Canton Official Patch". High margin, zero stock risk.
  • Discreet Support: A digital "Financial Assistance" channel to retain students during tough times.
  • Centralised Feedback: AI-assisted categorisation of issues, delivered to the right person instantly.
