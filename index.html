THE FUTURE OF OUR DOJOS

Applying the Japanese philosophy of "Kaizen" (Continuous Improvement) to our Dojo operations.

Our Foundation: Core Values

SPIRIT

We never give up, we never give in, we will always do our best.

QUALITY

Classes are engaging, evolving, applicable and inclusive.

SERVICE

We will offer a premium experience through premium customer service.

DEDICATION

Our aim is to inspire others to become dedicated to their chosen sport.

FUN

We ensure you enjoy the journey; a smile on your face is what we want.

Strategic Objectives

1. The Power of Expression

Granting the formal right to be heard reduces internal tension and fosters a sense of belonging and ownership.

2. The Innovation Bridge

Cross-Dojo statistics and idea sharing create a permanent bridge, accelerating friendship beyond competition mats.

3. The Official Patch Model

Allow comfortable WKF-approved gear but mandate a high-quality "Canton Official Velcro Patch" for club identity.

4. 360° Event Lifecycle

Mandatory pre/post-event digital feedback ensures every voice is heard, preventing social media venting.

5. AI-Assisted Efficiency

Digital categorisation routes issues instantly to the right department, ensuring no problem remains unaddressed.

6. Digital Process Integration

Fully digital workflows for applications, payments, and reviews eliminate data loss and streamline administration.

7. Confidential "Digital Voice"

A discreet, encrypted channel allows members to request financial or safeguarding support without fear.

8. Centralised Management Line

A direct line to the Review Board ensures accountability. Every valid suggestion reaches the decision-makers.

Success Metric

We will measure success by the Net Retention Rate following a 1-Month Pilot Phase.

Target: Verify if "The Opportunity to Express" directly correlates to "Long-term Loyalty".

The Patch Strategy

PROBLEM

Sizing issues and stock costs with mandatory club Gis lead to student dissatisfaction and revenue loss.

SOLUTION

The "Canton Official Velcro Patch" — a high-quality embroidered badge students attach to any WKF-approved gear.

OUTCOME

Students purchase comfortable, quality gear (satisfaction) and wear the patch (identity). Results in higher profit margins and happiness.
